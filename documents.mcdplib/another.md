\begin{example}
\label{exa:one}Consider the CDPI in \figref{Simple-DP}. The \uline{m}inimal
resources~$M\subseteq\Aressp$ are the objectives of this optimization
problem:
\[
M\doteq\begin{cases}
\with & \fun,\res\in\funsp=\ressp,\\
\Min_{\posleq} & \res,\\
 & \res\in\ftor(\fun),\\
 & \res\posleq\fun.
\end{cases}
\]
\end{example}
